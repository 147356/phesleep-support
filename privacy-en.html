Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Core Commitment: Phesleep does not collect any user data. No account registration required. Your privacy is the top priority.

1. I Do Not Collect Any Data

Phesleep does not collect, store, or upload any user data:

  • No account registration required
  • No personal information collected
  • No physiological data (heart rate, sleep cycles, etc.)
  • No sleep monitoring
  • No usage time tracking
  • No location information

2. Local Storage

  • Fully Local: Voice packs and preferences are stored only on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: No data is uploaded to the cloud
  • Offline Use: After downloading voice packs, the app works completely offline

3. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash information to improve stability

I do not sell or share your personal information with any third parties.

4. Contact & Feedback

If you contact me via email, I will receive the content of your email, which is used only to respond to your questions.
