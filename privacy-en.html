Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Core Commitment: Phesleep follows the "Data Minimization" principle. I do not collect any physiological data. Your privacy is the top priority.

1. Information I Collect

I adhere to minimal data collection principles:

  • Account Information: Only your email address if you choose to register (optional)
  • Usage Preferences: Your selected voice, language settings (stored locally)
  • Device Information: Basic device model information for app compatibility

I Do NOT Collect:

  • Any physiological data (heart rate, sleep cycles, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Contacts or other personal data

2. How I Use Information

The limited information collected is only used to:

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Save your personalized settings
  • Provide technical support services

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage First: Voice packs and preferences are stored on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: I do not upload your usage data to the cloud
  • Offline Use: After downloading voice packs, the app works completely offline

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Crash Reporting: Anonymous crash information to improve stability

I do not sell your personal information to any third parties.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and export your data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of any optional data collection

To exercise these rights, please contact: phesleep@outlook.com

6. Contact Me

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Email: phesleep@outlook.com
