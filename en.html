Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the voice guidance sound?

Go to "Profile" page, tap "Voice Settings", where you can select different voice tones and languages.

Where are downloaded voice packs stored?

Voice packs are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to the cloud, protecting your privacy.

Why does the voice guidance gradually become quieter?

This is Phesleep's core design philosophy "Monotonic Decrease" ‚Äî as you gradually fall asleep, the sound naturally fades until it disappears.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, once you download voice packs, you can use the app completely offline without any network connection.
